Functions
  • Complete window manager functions: Minimise, maximise, and movable windows. The taskbar items also fully work

Internet Explorer

You can visit most sites by simply typing the URL into the address bar of Internet Explorer and hitting enter.

  Tip: This is IE 1.0, you can't search in the address bar like modern browsers! You need a URL.

Only the Home, Stop and Reload buttons work. The other either would've been impossible, or too complex and outside the scope of this Code Jam.

And for the sites biggest feature: Webpages are Parsed by the server to make them look older! Fonts, backgrounds, borders and more are all changed! Google and the Apple site look particularly good.
